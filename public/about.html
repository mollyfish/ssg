KnitWare

What is KnitWare?

The purpose of KnitWare is to provide knitters with an easy way to design custom projects anywhere, anytime! Right now, it is a bit limited, but stay tuned! Updates will be added soon, including lots of customization options, a smart-phone friendly edition, and even men's sizes!

Where did KnitWare come from?

KnitWare got its start as PROJECT:Knittable, the capstone project for a coding bootcamp. Molly, a knitter-turned-programmer, had the original idea, and still works on the program today.
